Your Tour Director

Practical, personable and easy to travel with.

My aim on tour is simple: keep things clear, keep things moving and make it easier for everyone to relax into the trip. Good communication and good pace make a bigger difference than overcomplicating everything.

I like tours to feel well organised, straightforward and personal without becoming over-managed. When the practical side is handled well, everyone enjoys the journey more.

I work across the UK, Ireland, Europe and Scandinavia, leading the kinds of trips that move through big landscapes, historic cities and plenty of memorable stops in between.

This site gives you a clearer starting point before departure: the useful details in one place, with a little more personality than a standard travel email.
